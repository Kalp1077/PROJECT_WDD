Died: 8 April 1973 (age 91 years), Mougins, France
Periods: Cubism, Modern art, Surrealism, Picasso's Blue Period, Modernism
Influenced by: Vincent van Gogh, Paul Cézanne, Diego Velázquez, Francisco Goya
Full name: Pablo Diego José Francisco de Paula Juan Nepomuceno María de los Remedios Cipriano de la Santísima Trinidad Ruiz y Picasso
Education: San Fernando Fine Art Royal Academy
